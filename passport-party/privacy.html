Passport Party

Privacy Policy

Passport Party is a local-device trivia and timing game. This page explains what the current launch build stores, what it does not collect, and how players can remove their saved data.

No account required No analytics SDK No ad tracking in this build

What this app stores

  • The nickname a player types into the app.
  • Local guest progress such as score, passport stamps, seen stops, and last saved run state.
  • Private-room draft details such as the room code currently shown on the device.

What this build does not do

  • It does not require sign-in or account creation.
  • It does not send nickname or guest progress to Passport Party servers.
  • It does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking in the current build.
  • It does not collect precise location, contacts, photos, or microphone data.

Sharing

If a player taps the share button for a private room, the app opens the system share sheet so the player can send the room code through another app they choose. Passport Party does not receive or store that message.

Data retention and deletion

  • Guest progress stays on the device until it is removed.
  • Using Reset Guest Save inside the app deletes the stored nickname and local progress.
  • Deleting the app also removes any remaining local app data from the device.

Children

Passport Party is intended for a general audience and is not designed to collect personal data from children.
